Batteleship Game Instructions

In Batteleship Game, Player challenge the Computer by using a 10×10 grid in virtual Battleship. The ships are randomly positioned, each opponent fire upon squares in the adverse grid and tries to sink their enemies fleet in turns. The program indicates automatically whether there has been a hit or a miss. The player who destroys the totality of their opponent’s fleet wins the game.
